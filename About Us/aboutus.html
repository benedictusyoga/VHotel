About Us

Learn more about the history of VhoTel and how it came to be how it is today.

History

The V group started their journey back in the 40s where our founder, Benedict Yog III started a company that provided linen cloths and toiletries to be used in the nearby hotels and motels alike.

As years passed, our founder decided to expand the V group to not only produce linen cloth, but also delve into the world of hotels. Thus, our first ever hotel, VhoTel Jakarta was opened.

Such that the world changes constantly, we also strive for progress here in V group. Until today, we have successfully brought elegance and comfort to 5 branches spread across Nusantara.

Now,

The V Group prides itself in being the leading candidate for vinta-legence (Vintage and Elegance) hotel in the heart of the city.
